Faro

Keep one daily promise, and a boat sails the real world map for you. Forgiving, never resets. Buy once, no subscription.

Faro turns a single promise to yourself into a calm voyage across real geography — port by port, chapter by chapter. Keep today and the boat sails on; miss a day and it simply waits at anchor. Collect countries in your passport, earn seals for finished chapters, and watch your lifelong Atlas fill in. Everything stays on your device. No account. No subscription.

Designed in Germany. Solo-developed. Coming to the App Store.

Roll

A photo curation tool for iOS. 100 % on-device. Your library, considered.

Roll sorts the strong shots from the weaker ones and prepares them for curation — so tending your library takes minutes, not hours. Bursts, blurry frames, screenshots, themed collections, all surfaced as one-tap decisions. Your photos never leave your iPhone.

Designed in Germany. Solo-developed. Coming to the App Store.

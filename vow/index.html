Vow

Keep your word.

Vow turns app-blocking into a ritual you actually keep. Choose a phase — Deep Work, Family time, Exercise, Sleep, or one of your own — and Vow blocks what distracts, backed by Apple's real Screen Time framework. Tap an NFC sticker to start or end a phase, and Vow can quietly turn on your linked Apple Focus too. Calm, no gamification, no account. Buy once, no subscription.

Designed in Germany. Solo-developed.

summa

Bank statements into clean spreadsheets.

Import a PDF bank statement and summa turns it into a clean Excel or CSV file — reconciled, categorised, ready for taxes and bookkeeping. Everything is read on your iPhone; your statements never leave your device. No bank login, no account, no cloud.

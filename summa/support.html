Frequently asked

Where are my statements stored?
Only on your iPhone. summa has no account and no cloud — delete the app and the data is gone. Export the spreadsheets you want to keep before removing the app.
Does my bank data leave my phone?
No. Every statement is read on-device and never uploaded. summa has no server that stores your documents.
Which formats can I export?
Excel (.xlsx), CSV for Excel (German or US/International), and a 3-column CSV for QuickBooks. Choose the format from the export menu on any statement.
What does "needs review" mean?
summa cross-checks each statement against its opening and closing balance. If a row doesn't add up, or the statement has no balance to check against, summa flags it so you can verify the numbers before exporting — it never shows a statement as clean unless it reconciles.
How do I unlock summa Pro?
The free tier converts one statement per month. summa Pro unlocks unlimited statements as a one-time Lifetime purchase or a yearly subscription. Restore Purchases is at the bottom of Settings.

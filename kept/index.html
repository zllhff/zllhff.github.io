kept

A quiet place for your papers. Privacy-first iOS document organizer. Buy once. Your family gets it.

kept scans, signs, and organizes your important documents — all on your iPhone. Apple Intelligence names every scan; Smart Stacks groups passports, receipts and contracts for you. Cancellation letters in DIN 5008 format. No account. No subscription. No server you don't control.

Designed in Germany. Solo-developed. Coming to the App Store.

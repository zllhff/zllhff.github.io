kept · Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-22 · Effective immediately.

kept runs on your iPhone. Your documents are stored and processed locally. iCloud sync is an optional Pro feature that you control. We don't operate any servers and we never see your documents. kept makes network calls only for purchases — see "Data we send to third parties".

Data we collect

No document data. Your files, their text, and everything kept derives from them stay on your device. We don't operate any servers and there is no kept user database.

For purchases and product improvement, a small, anonymous amount of usage data goes to our purchase provider — see the next section.

Data we send to third parties

kept uses RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc., USA) to process and manage "kept pro" purchases. What goes there:

  • an anonymous, randomly generated identifier — no name, no email, no Apple ID, no advertising ID (IDFA);
  • your purchase status for "kept pro" (purchased, restored, refunded);
  • three anonymous usage values: which feature opened the purchase screen (first time and most recent), and how many days ago the app was installed;
  • the Apple Search Ads attribution token, if you found kept through an Apple ad — provided by Apple, with no personal reference.

Purpose: understanding which features lead to a purchase, and restoring purchases across your devices. Document contents, titles, categories, amounts, and free text are never transmitted — the usage values come from a fixed list of feature names in the code.

No cross-app tracking, no ad networks, no sale to data brokers. Under Apple's definition this is not "tracking", which is why kept never shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

Separately, Apple receives the anonymous MetricKit summary it aggregates from every iOS app — we never receive it.

What lives on your device

  • The original files you import — PDFs, images, scans, notes — stored in a secure App Group container shared between the main app and the Share Sheet extension.
  • Thumbnails, OCR text, and AI-extracted metadata (titles, categories, monetary amounts) — generated on-device using Apple's Vision, NaturalLanguage and Foundation Models frameworks.
  • Your categories, spaces, stacks, actions, and signatures.
  • Your settings (accent color, layout preference, sort, etc.) in iOS standard storage.

All of the above can be wiped at any time by deleting the app or via Settings → kept → Reset.

iCloud Sync (optional, Pro feature)

If you enable iCloud sync (a kept pro feature), kept uses Apple's CloudKit to synchronize your documents across your own Apple devices. This data lives in your private iCloud container, end-to-end encrypted by Apple. We do not have access to it. We do not see it. We do not store it on any kept-operated infrastructure (there is none).

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in Settings → kept → iCloud Sync. Local-only mode is always available.

Apple Intelligence (optional, on-device)

On iPhone 15 Pro and newer, kept uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to suggest document titles, categories, and stack groupings. All AI processing happens on your device. No document text ever leaves your iPhone for AI processing. On older devices, kept falls back to local keyword heuristics.

Family Sharing

kept pro is enabled for Apple Family Sharing — your purchase unlocks kept pro for up to 5 family members in your iCloud Family Group. Family Sharing only shares the app purchase, not your documents. Each family member uses their own iPhone, their own iCloud, their own kept library. We never see family relationships, member names, or who-shared-what.

Apple Reminders integration (optional, Pro)

If you connect kept to Apple Reminders (an optional Pro feature), kept creates reminders for due-date actions. This integration uses Apple's EventKit framework — we never see your reminders, only push entries via Apple's API.

Children's privacy

kept does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. The purchase and usage values described above are anonymous and cannot be traced back to a person.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the change date above will be revised. We will not retroactively change the policy to collect data that wasn't collected before.

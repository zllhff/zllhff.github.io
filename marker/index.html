Marker

Your lab results, tracked on your iPhone.

Import a lab report and Marker lays every value next to the reference range printed on that same report, then keeps a tidy history over time. Everything is read on your iPhone; your lab values never leave your device. No account, no cloud, no sign-in.

  • Four ways to import: PDF, the iOS share sheet, your camera, or manual entry.
  • A clear history and trend for each marker over time.
  • A safe, checked read: values shown only against the range on your own report.

Marker is for informational use only and is not a medical device. It does not provide a diagnosis, medical advice, or treatment recommendations. Always talk to a qualified healthcare professional about your results.
